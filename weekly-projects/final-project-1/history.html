    The first passport was accredited to King Henry V of England, but they
    were not widely used until right after WW1, in 1918


    Each country, in addition to some individual cities within the country have
    their own stamp. So, depending on the origin of the stamp, it may reveal not
    only the country, but also the city within the country. The appearance of the
    stamps depends on the country you are in.


    Entrance and exit stamps. 


    Passports can not only provide information to the authorities of the country which
    you are entering into, but also provide a memory of pass travels that you can use
    for personal enjoyment. 


    Passport stamps are not in any specific order.
