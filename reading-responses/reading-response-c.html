Reading Response C:

"A collection is usually an attempt to someone take hold of the world around us, to control atleast a few of its parts."

I thought this quote introduced an interesting way to think about the
relationship between the world and specifically between an artist/designer
and their work and the ways in which this reflects life. If you think about
A collection as an attempt to take hold of the world, then I think its acceptable
to say that an artist work is perhaps a reflection of what they do not yet have hold
of or of what they would like to obtain a clearer viewpoint about.
